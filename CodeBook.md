## Data
- Data obtained from: http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones
- Data for the project: https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip

## Identifier
- Subject: Contain 30 rows each representing a participant in the study
- Activity: Type of activity performed when the corresponding measurements were taken

## Description of Activitity_Labels.txt
- **WALKING** (value = 1): Subject was walking during the test
- **WALKING_UPSTAIRS** (value = 2): Subject was walking up a staircase during the test
- **WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS** (value = 3): Subject was walking down a staircase during the test
- **SITTING** (value = 4): Subject was sitting during the test
- **STANDING** (value = 5): Subject was standing during the test
- **LAYING** (value = 6): Subject was laying down during the test
